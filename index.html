African Peace Organisation is an NGO with an aim of building sustainable hope and development for African community using rebuilding love and peace program as per their natural character. It has continuously worked with various communities, civil society Organisations, local leaders, government bodies and enginners to promote a sustainable, equitable and participatory changes and development in communities.

Our Vision

To actively promote African Peace and Contribute to the socio-economic development of communities in which we operate and to be an Organisation that continually responds to the changing social realities through development and application of knowledge, towards a sustainable society that promotes human rights, gender equality, social justice and freedoms

Our Mission

To work towards the promotion of the aims and objectives of already existing government initiatives put in place for the benefit of the community and the Organisation structures and initiatives that facilitate the development of our communities through social work, research and design reach out programs locally, nationally and regionally to address different challenges faced in communities

Issues of interest

  • APO is concerned with working in education and health forums, human rights initiatives, orphans and women, disabled and elderly persons, poor communities, jobless youth and street childrem development programs for the betterment of the communities and individual beneficiaries.
  • Other concerns include: sanitation and agriculture development, Rular electification, poverty alleviation initiatives and child abuse in communities

Our main Objectives

Counselling and Conflict Resolution, the project aims at giving people free and adequate Counselling to support their mental and moral well being including follow up activities

Mass awareness programs and advocacy, where relevant information and sensitization concerning community wellbeing and developments are passed on through mass media platformsand written materials be ing distributed in communities.
